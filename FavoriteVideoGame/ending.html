Final Mission: Midnight

In the final mission, Midnight, Master Chief boards the Didact’s ship, Mantle’s Approach, in orbit above Earth. With Cortana’s assistance, he fights through Promethean forces to stop the Didact from activating the Composer against humanity.

The Epilogue

Cortana uses the last of her strength to disable the Didact’s defenses and protect Master Chief from the Composer’s blast. She sacrifices herself to ensure humanity’s survival. After the battle, Chief returns to Earth and reflects on Cortana’s loss, ending the game on an emotional and personal note.
