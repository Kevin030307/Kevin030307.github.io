About Halo 4

Halo 4 is a first-person shooter video game developed by 343 Industries and released in 2012 for the Xbox 360. It is the first game in the Reclaimer Saga and follows the legendary Spartan super-soldier Master Chief as he awakens from cryosleep aboard the drifting UNSC Forward Unto Dawn. The game introduces a more emotional and personal story, focusing on Master Chief's relationship with his AI companion, Cortana.

Game Features
